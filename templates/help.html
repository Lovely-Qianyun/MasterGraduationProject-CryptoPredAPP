How to use the Cryptocurrency Price Prediction System

Getting Started

Step 1: Select Cryptocurrency

Choose from the available cryptocurrencies:

  • Bitcoin - The original and most popular cryptocurrency
  • Ethereum - Platform for smart contracts and DApps
  • Litecoin - Fast and low-cost digital currency
  • Dogecoin - Community-driven meme coin

Step 2: Choose Prediction Model

Select from our advanced machine learning models:

  • CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) - Good for pattern recognition in price data
  • ConvLSTM - Combines CNN and LSTM for spatiotemporal patterns
  • EDLSTM (Encoder-Decoder LSTM) - Sequence-to-sequence learning for time series
  • Transformer - State-of-the-art attention mechanism for long-term dependencies

Step 3: Set Prediction Period

Choose how many days ahead you want to predict:

  • 7 days - Short-term predictions with higher accuracy
  • 15 days - Medium-term forecasting
  • 30 days - Long-term trend analysis

Understanding Results

The prediction results include:

  • Historical Prices - Blue line showing past 30 days
  • Predicted Prices - Red dashed line showing future predictions
  • Price Summary - Detailed prices for the first few predicted days

Important Notes
